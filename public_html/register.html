PRACTICE TEAM

Welcome to the official practice team of Muskegon Risers SC! Per NCAA guidelines, amateur players are allowed to train with professional teams, so long as the player pays for training as an extended service of the professional team. As a practice team player, your name will not appear on our roster. You will, however, be exposed to high level training with the coaches and players of Muskegon Risers SC. Please complete your registration below.

Price: $300
