OUR PURPOSE

Muskegon Risers Soccer Club is an independent men’s soccer team that will begin competition in May of 2015. The highest priority of the organization is continuing the growth and excitement surrounding soccer in the greater Muskegon area – defined as Whitehall, Muskegon, and Grand Haven. The Risers will strive to be a representation of local soccer through both on-field performance and off-field community service. Our vision is to be a unifying force between area students, professionals, and families alike. Through the game of soccer, we have a distinct opportunity to generate a lasting social and economic impact across the greater Muskegon community.

It is important to communicate the creative vision behind Muskegon Risers SC. The Risers lime/royal color scheme is designed to reflect our area's natural beauty - which includes Lake Michigan, park forests, and the inland lakes which shape Whitehall, Muskegon, and Grand Haven. What is a Riser? A person of the Muskegon area. Someone who understands the area's potential and who pushes themselves and others to realize that potential. The name is not limited to an era or industry. Muskegon Risers SC strives to represent the spirit of our area's people in past, present, and future tense.

MEET OUR STAFF




Muskegon Yoga Center

Muskegon Yoga Center is the official Yoga Partner of Muskegon Risers SC and has been a premier yoga studio on the West Michigan Lakeshore for the past six years. Muskegon Yoga is committed to enhancing the health and lifestyle of Risers players by providing a high quality, safe, and fun yoga experience. The inspiring instructors of Muskegon Yoga Center are at the core of its success. Each instructor brings expertise, knowledge and yoga love with RYT 200 level certification. We are excited to partner with Muskegon Yoga Center and look forward to the positive impact their instructors will have on player performance.


Dr. Paul Kimbro, OD

Dr. Paul Kimbro is the Team Optometrist for Muskegon Risers SC. In addition to being an academic stud throughout high school and college, Dr. Kimbro excelled on the soccer field and hockey rink. A Resident Optometrist at Henry Ford OptomEyes in West Bloomfield, MI, Dr. Kimbro's optometric expertise and athletic experience will combine to serve Risers players with the highest level of eye care. Optimal vision is essential for athlete success, and we are thrilled to boast Dr. Kimbro as a member of the Risers staff.


Dr. Mark Peterman, PT, DPT

Dr. Mark Peterman is the official Team Physical Therapist for Muskegon Risers SC. A life-long soccer player and fan, Dr. Peterman graduated from Western Michigan Christian to pursue a career in physical therapy. After earning a Bachelor of Science degree from Grand Valley State University in Health Science, Dr. Peterman landed at Franklin Pierce College in Manchester, NH just outside of Boston to further his education and graduate with a Doctorate in Physical Therapy. Dr. Peterman is back in the greater Muskegon area, currently residing in Grand Haven and working within Mercy Health Partners.


Dr. Gretchen Goltz, DO

Dr. Gretchen Goltz is the Team Physician for Muskegon Risers SC, providing Risers players access to exceptional primary healthcare through her Muskegon-based medical practice, Norton Family Practice, and Mercy Health Partners. Dr. Goltz earned a Doctorate in Osteopathy from Michigan State University's School of Medicine after studying on the lakeshore at Hope College in Holland. Finally, Dr. Goltz is a soccer mom, which makes her a perfect fit for the Risers staff.


Dr. Mike Morea

Dr. Mike Morea is the official Team Chiropractor for Muskegon Risers SC. Dr. Morea has extensive experience working with athletes and has a passion for working with the human body. Dr. Mike Morea graduated with a degree in biology from Michigan State University and graduated from Chiropractic School at Life University in Georgia earning top honors, Summa Cum Laude. He is very excited to be working with our local soccer team and looks forward to helping each player achieve optimal performance under chiropractic care. Dr. Mike resides in the Norton Shores area with his wife Nicole, and their four children.


Dr. Peter W. Blackburn, DDS, PLC

Dr. Blackburn is the Team Dentist for Muskegon Risers SC. For nearly 17 years, Dr. Blackburn has been providing quality dental care to families in the Muskegon area. Dr. Blackburn's goal is to support the team's needs with the utmost comfort. In addition to minimizing wait times in his reception area, Dr. Blackburn will schedule extra time for appointments to ensure dental needs and concerns are properly addressed. We are excited to have the traditional "small town" feel of Dr. Blackburn and his team partnered with Muskegon Risers SC.




Salim Al-Shatel

Salim represents Muskegon Risers SC as a Partnership Coordinator in the Muskegon Market. An engineering student at Michigan State University, Salim plans to pursue a career in professional sports as an agent following graduation. We look forward to observing the positive impact Salim will have on the Risers organization.

PROJECT RISING

Project Rising is a community outreach program set to launch in May of 2015. The single purpose of Project Rising is to make a positive impact on the Muskegon lakeshore through soccer. Efforts will largely focus on establishing soccer programs for youth in the urban neighborhoods of Muskegon and Muskegon Heights. Please donate or list yourself as an interested volunteer for this cause.
