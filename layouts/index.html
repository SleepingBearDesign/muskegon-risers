Our Purpose

Muskegon Risers Soccer Club is an independent men’s soccer team that will begin competition in May of 2015. The highest priority of the organization is continuing the growth and excitement surrounding soccer in the greater Muskegon area – defined as Whitehall, Muskegon, and Grand Haven. The Risers will strive to be a representation of local soccer through both on-field performance and off-field community service. Our vision is to be a unifying force between area students, professionals, and families alike. Through the game of soccer, we have a distinct opportunity to generate a lasting social and economic impact across the greater Muskegon community.

It is important to communicate the creative vision behind Muskegon Risers SC. The Risers lime/royal color scheme is designed to reflect our area's natural beauty - which includes Lake Michigan, park forests, and the inland lakes which shape Whitehall, Muskegon, and Grand Haven. What is a Riser? A person of the Muskegon area. Someone who understands the area's potential and who pushes themselves and others to realize that potential. The name is not limited to an era or industry. Muskegon Risers SC strives to represent the spirit of our area's people in past, present, and future tense.
